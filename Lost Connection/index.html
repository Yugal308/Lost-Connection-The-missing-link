How it Works

A Guide To find Your Loved Ones

01

FOUND A MISSING PERSON/CHILD

If you come across someone who’s been lost or if you have lost someone, access this portal for help.

02

OBTAIN INFORMATION (FACIAL RECOGNITION)

Fill up the registration form to provide the details of the found/lost person in detail and upload there image or fingerprint. The image/fingerprint uploaded is searched through the missing people’s database.

03

ALERT

If a match of the persons’s details is found, an alert is sent to the respective guardians and police station.
